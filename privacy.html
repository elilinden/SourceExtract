Privacy Policy for SourceExtract

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled when you use the SourceExtract iOS application ("the App").

1. Data Collection and Usage

SourceExtract is designed with privacy as a fundamental priority. The App operates entirely locally on your device as a web browser and media extraction tool.

We do not collect, store, or transmit your browsing history, search queries, bookmarks, or the media you download.

No External Servers: The App does not send any of your personal data or browsing activity to any external servers or third-party analytics services.

2. Local Storage

All data generated by your use of the App remains strictly on your iOS device.

Downloaded Media: Images and videos you choose to collect are saved directly to your device's native Photo Library or to your local Files app, depending on your selected preferences.

App Data: Your active tabs, bookmarks, and local browsing history are stored using native Apple frameworks (such as UserDefaults and local file storage) and never leave your device. You can clear this data at any time within the App's settings.

3. Permissions

To function properly, SourceExtract may request the following native iOS permissions:

Photo Library Access: Required only if you choose to save extracted media directly to your Photos app. The App only writes to your library and does not scan or transmit your existing photos.

4. Third-Party Websites

SourceExtract functions as a web browser. By using the App, you will navigate to and interact with third-party websites. This Privacy Policy does not apply to those external sites, and we are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, or data collection methods.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."
