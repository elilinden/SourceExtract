Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why didn't an image or video download?

Some websites use aggressive anti-scraping techniques, restrict media behind logins, or load content dynamically (lazy loading). Make sure the page has fully loaded and scroll through the page before tapping the collect button.

Where are my downloads saved?

You can save media directly to your iOS Photo Library or to a dedicated "SourceExtract" folder in your Files app. Permissions can be managed in your iOS Settings.

Why are some images skipped during deduplication?

SourceExtract identifies duplicates based on exact source URLs. If the same image is hosted at different URLs, both may appear. However, identical URLs are only shown once to keep results clean and organized.
